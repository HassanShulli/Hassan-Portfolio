Project

Billboard Management System

A nationwide platform for managing static and digital billboards across Malaysia — from proposals to invoices.

Overview

A management system for advertising agencies to manage billboards across Malaysia. Billboards are added to the platform and clients can select the ones they wish to use and add their media. The agency generates a proposal which, once approved by the client, launches a campaign — with invoices and receipts generated automatically in the campaign module.

Key Features

  • Analytics page showing billboard, campaign, and user activity on the platform.
  • Invoice module for generating invoices, debit notes, and credit notes.
  • Campaign module for uploading billboard media, creating proposals, and generating receipts.
  • Billboard inventory management across multiple locations nationwide.
